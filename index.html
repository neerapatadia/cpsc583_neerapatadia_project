Understanding Canada-wide Cancer Incidence Data from 2013 to 2017

A CPSC 583 project by Neera Patadia

Project Overview

Cancer is currently one of the leading causes of death in Canada.

To understand the impact of cancer on the population, we can analyze large-scale datasets.

The goal of this project is to analyze and understand trends in cancer incidence and diagnosis from 2013-2017 across Canada.

We focus on looking data from:

  • Lung Cancer
  • Colorectal Cancer
  • Breast Cancer
  • Cervical Cancer
  • Prostate Cancer
